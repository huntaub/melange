Link Account Step 1 of 2

To begin, you need to go to a computer that has access to your current account. Open Melange, and navigate to Settings. Then click the Identity tab at the top of the screen.

Finally, click the identity that you wish to link, then click the "Start Link" button. A screen will open that will inform you it is waiting for a link initiation. Once that has occurred, enter your current address below.
